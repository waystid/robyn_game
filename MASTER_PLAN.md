## ðŸ› Common Issues & Solutions

**Solution:** Check Unity version matches (2022.3 LTS)

### "Survival Engine import errors"
**Solution:** Click "Ignore" or "Continue", then try Assets â†’ Reimport All

### "My character won't move"
**Solution:**
1. Check PlayerController is attached
2. Verify ground has collider

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Success

### Mindset
1. **Start simple** - Don't try to make it perfect
2. **Test often** - Click Play every 15 minutes
3. **Use placeholders** - Cubes are fine for testing
4. **Ask for help** - I'm here to write all your code!
5. **Have fun** - This is your creative project!

### Unity Tips
1. **Save frequently** - Ctrl+S every 5 minutes
2. **Use scenes** - Save different versions for testing
3. **Organize hierarchy** - Use empty GameObjects as folders
4. **Name things clearly** - "Player", "Dragon_NPC", not "GameObject (3)"

### Development Tips
1. **Follow schedule** - Don't jump ahead or get distracted
2. **One feature at a time** - Complete before moving on
3. **Copy/paste code carefully** - Don't skip lines
4. **Read error messages** - They tell you what's wrong

### Time Management
1. **2-4 hours per day** is ideal
2. **Take breaks** every hour
3. **Don't rush** - quality over speed
4. **Skip optional tasks** if behind schedule
